Lake Bunyonyi

Lake Bunyonyi ”Place of many little birds” lies in south western Uganda in Kabale district. The lake is the deepest in Uganda and is the second deepest lake in Africa. It is a magical spot that has been renamed the ”Switzerland of Africa” and as its name ”Bunyonyi” suggests in the local language, the lake is a haven for bird watchers. The lake has got distinct bird species which include a variety of Herons, grey crowned cranes, egrets, weaverbirds among others. More so it’s probably the best place in Africa to see otters-the diurnal spotted-necked otter.
